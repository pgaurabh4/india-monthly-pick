# India Monthly Pick

One high-conviction Indian stock per month. The idea is simple: find a small or mid cap company that is in an excellent structural position to benefit from India's growth story over the next decade, is NOT a value trap, and offers an attractive entry point right now. Buy it. Don't touch it for 5–10 years.

## Rules
- **1 pick per month**, no more
- Must be **small cap or mid cap** (₹2,000–30,000 Cr market cap) with realistic large cap potential
- Must be in a sector aligned with **India's structural growth drivers**
- Forward PE must be **attractive relative to growth** (not cheap for a reason)
- **Not a value trap** — growth story must be backed by order book, contracts, or structural tailwinds, not hope
- 5–10 year time horizon. Set and forget.
